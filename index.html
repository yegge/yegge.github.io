ABOUT YEGGE

Yegge is an independent music label founded by Brian Yegge, dedicated to pushing the boundaries of electronic and experimental music through two distinctive projects: Angershade and The Corruptive.

Our mission is to create music that challenges conventional thinking and explores the intersection of technology, emotion, and human experience.

THE PROJECTS

ANGERSHADE

Angershade represents the darker, more introspective side of electronic music, creating atmospheric soundscapes that delve into themes of isolation, technology, and the human condition.

THE CORRUPTIVE

The Corruptive explores experimental territories, breaking down traditional musical structures to rebuild them in unexpected ways, creating music that corrupts and transforms conventional expectations.
